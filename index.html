The origin

Years of watching good operations fail at the seams.

We spent years inside industrial operations — one of us managing maintenance across automated distribution systems, the other servicing warehouse equipment in the field. Different roles, same view: skilled people wasting hours on coordination work, dispatchers holding operations together with phone calls and sticky notes, and information falling through the gaps between systems that were never designed to talk to each other.

We each built internal tools that fixed real friction — and watched how much value one well-designed system could unlock. Most businesses are still running their operations on email, memory, and hope. Not because better isn’t possible. Because nobody built it for them.

So we built the system we wished existed. This is it.

The problem we solve

Not another AI product. An actual answer to an actual problem.

The tools that exist today fall into two camps: tools that help individuals do specific tasks faster, and enterprise software that takes months to implement and requires an IT department to maintain. Neither is what most operators need.

Most operators need the work to move without them having to push it. They need the system to remember what was said, follow up when nothing happens, and put a human in the loop only when actually needed. iNTELLiDOCK Ai is built for that problem — the one most businesses are solving with email, memory, and hope.
